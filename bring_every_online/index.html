About Us

Welcome to Mohan Store, your trusted source for premium bananas and delectable bakery items for over 20 years! Specializing in organic bananas sourced directly from local farmers, our commitment to quality and freshness is unmatched. Our diverse banana varieties cater to every taste, while our bakery delights, crafted with passion, promise a symphony of flavors. As a family-owned establishment, we take pride in being a community cornerstone, offering you the finest produce and baked goods with a legacy of excellence that spans two decades. Thank you for choosing Mohan stores, where every bite tells a story of quality, tradition, and the joy of savoring life's simple pleasures.
